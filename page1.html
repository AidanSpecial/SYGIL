About Us

What is SYGIL?

SYGIL is a high end fashion and engineering line that combines designer styling with protective armor. SYGIL's philosophy is that all clothing should be functional as well as being beautiful.

Is SYGIL right for me?

SYGIL is perfect for anyone who understands the importance of protection in our ever-changing world. Not just for themselves, but for their loved ones as well. We design garments that make you look good, feel good, and you'll actually want to wear, while protecting you.

Why SYGIL?

SYGIL's team has over fifteen years experience in textiles. This experience allows us to design high fashion garments that are also functional. SYGIL's team can work with you to create custom garments for personal use, for a customer, or to outfit your team.
